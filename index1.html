Dessert

Types of Desserts

There are many types of desserts. There are cookies, pastries, cakes, pies, ice creams, and more! Desserts can taste like fruit, chcolate, vanilla, cinnamon, caramel, nuts, and a variety of other flavors!

Who Makes Desserts

Anybody can make desserts! They are fun to make and enjoy. Someone who professionally makes desserts is called a baker or a pastry chef.

Why Desserts are Good

Desserts are fun to make, and taste great! They are a wonderful addition to any social event, and an amazing treat for after a meal.
